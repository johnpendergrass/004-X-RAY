  • All pages expect at HD browser res (ie. 1920x1080 or more).

  • I wanted to see what use -hover- could be in revealing info....

    1. This could be useful in an online 'point and click' dungeon crawl game. I know it would be implemented better, more efficient.
    2. There are three layers of images:
      • Background image, of gold, monster, whatever
      • Foregound image, which covers the background completely, except for some 'cutouts' (transparent areas) that you will want so display the background through
      • the 'doors' or 'windows', which cover the 'cutouts' of the foreground image. These will get -hover- and -opacity- settings to allow them to show the background through when user hovers over them.
